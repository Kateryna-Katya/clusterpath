About the Game

It's basically a really fast strategy game
where you're constantly making split-second deck decisions

Castle Crush combines collectible card mechanics with real-time battlefield action.
Players summon units, manage energy, and adapt strategies during live battles against opponents from around the world.
We want it to be competitive enough for hardcore players but not so complex that casual gamers get overwhelmed.
